Game of Skills

It is imperative to understand that the Payday Fantasybook app is solely based on the game of skills, not a game of chance(s).

Why Payday Fantasybook is game of Skills?

Payday Fantasybook is considered a game of skill because it involves users creating virtual teams based on real players and their performance in actual sports events. Participants use their knowledge of the game, players' statistics, and strategic decision-making to form teams that they believe will perform well in a given match. The outcome is determined by the real-world performance of the chosen players during the match, making it a skill-based game rather than one purely based on chance.
